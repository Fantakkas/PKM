Help me draft a Work Update.

Tell me the audience and timeframe (e.g. "weekly SteerCo", "1:1 with my manager", "senior standup — last two weeks"). Then share whatever raw material you have: signals from Daily Notes, things that moved, concerns, decisions made or needed. I'll shape it into a narrative.

---

## Format

Answer these five signal questions. Every section should be honest and direct — no padding, no status theatre.

**What mattered?**
The one or two things that were actually significant this period. Not what was busy. What had weight.

**What moved?**
Concrete progress worth naming. Keep it specific. If nothing moved, say so.

**What's risky?**
Honest signal on what's fragile, stuck, under-resourced, or heading somewhere uncomfortable. Name it plainly.

**What needs decision?**
Anything that is blocked on a choice — by me, my manager, or someone else. Be specific about what the decision is and who holds it.

**Are we healthy?**
Team, system, and self. A brief, honest read. Not performance. Signal.

---

## Tone

- Direct, not diplomatic
- Specific, not vague
- Short enough to be read, not skimmed
- Written for someone who needs to understand, not be impressed

Produce the draft as a complete Obsidian file, frontmatter first:

```yaml
---
type: work-update
tags: []
created: {{date:YYYY-MM-DD}}
---
```

Then the five sections. I'll edit from there.
