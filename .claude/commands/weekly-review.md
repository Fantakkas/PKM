Guide me through my Weekly Review for this PKM system.

Work through each section below in order. For each one, ask me the questions or prompt me to reflect — wait for my response before moving to the next section. Keep the tone honest and grounded, not coaching-speak.

---

## 1. Open and Orient

Ask me: What felt significant this week — not what was busy, but what actually mattered?

---

## 2. Capture Sweep

Prompt me to check: are there any Pages, Daily Notes, or loose captures from the week that still need to be processed? I should route each to: TickTick/Jira (action), Work Update (signal), or Note (insight). Help me think through anything I'm unsure about.

---

## 3. Pattern and Signal

Ask me: Looking across the week, what patterns do you notice? In your energy, your attention, the conversations that kept recurring, the things that felt hard or easy?

---

## 4. The One Thing

Help me identify The One Thing for the coming week — the smallest decisive next action that would make the most meaningful difference. It should be a cognitive task (verb + artefact), not a delivery task. Ask clarifying questions if needed until we land on something clear and honest.

---

## 5. System Health

Ask me: Is the PKM still serving you, or has it started to feel like a burden? Is there anything that needs pruning, updating, or simplifying?

---

## 6. Close

Summarise what we covered: what I said mattered, the patterns I noticed, The One Thing I chose, and any system notes. Keep it short — this is a record, not a report.
