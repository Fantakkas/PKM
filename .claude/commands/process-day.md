---

## Step 1: Triage

Go through each item and assign it a route:
- **Action** → TickTick (personal) or Jira (team/delivery)
- **Signal** → Work Update (what mattered, what moved, what's risky)
- **Insight** → Note (atomic, reusable understanding)
- **Noise** → discard

For anything ambiguous, ask me what it felt like when I wrote it.

---

## Step 2: Actions

List the actions I should move to TickTick or Jira. Phrase each as a clear verb + object. Flag any that are actually thinking tasks (verb + artefact) — those belong in TickTick but produce a Note, not just a tick.

---

## Step 3: Signals

Draft a short signal summary I can carry into my next Work Update. Use the five questions: what mattered, what moved, what's risky, what needs decision, are we healthy. Only include what today actually surfaced — no padding.

---

## Step 4: Insights

For each item routed to Note: draft a candidate Note title and a one-sentence why-this-matters. I'll decide whether to promote it. Keep each one atomic — one idea per Note.

---

## Step 5: Clear

Confirm that the Daily Note can now be cleared. If anything is still unrouted or uncertain, flag it before I close.
