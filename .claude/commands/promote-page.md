Help me promote a Page to a Note.

Paste the Page content below, or describe what it contains. I'll guide you through the promotion process.

---

## Step 1: Is it ready?

Ask: Does this feel like something worth thinking with again, or is it still raw capture? If it's not ready, say so — Pages don't have to become Notes.

---

## Step 2: Rewrite

Help me rewrite the core idea in my own words — not a copy, not a summary, but the version I'd write if explaining it to myself in three months. Strip the scaffolding (meeting context, source phrasing). Keep only the idea.

---

## Step 3: Why this matters

Ask: Why does this matter to you specifically? What does it connect to? What would be worse if you forgot it? Draft a one or two sentence "why this matters" statement.

---

## Step 4: Links

Suggest at least one link — to another Note, a Person, a System, a Project, or an Area this connects to. Ask if there are others I'm aware of. A Note with no links is a dead end.

---

## Step 5: Stage

Set the Stage as Inbox. It will become Evergreen only when it has been used — when it appears in another Note, a Work Update, or a decision. Don't promote it to Evergreen now.

---

## Step 6: Draft

Produce the final Note draft:
- Title (clear, specific, in my own words — not the source's phrasing)
- Body (the idea, rewritten)
- Why this matters (one or two sentences)
- Source Page (link or reference)
- Stage: Inbox
- Suggested links

No meta-commentary. Just the Note.
